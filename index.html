A full-stack application that allows users to craft their own moodboard and view other's posts on its feed and save their posts to their own board. Users can manage their to-do lists and budget. A full-stack application that mimics most of the features Instagram. Features include: make an account, upload posts with pictures, look at a feed of all user posts, comment on posts, and view user profiles. Tech used: EJS, Node, MongoDB, Tailwind, Mongoose, and Passport

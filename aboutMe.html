About Me

I am a girl who loves to solve puzzles! I am always thinking about code and this passion often leads me to solve my coding problem. While I'm either swimming, playing bass, or studying a new language, I always create a solution to my problem and then run to my computer to test it.

My name is Shailyn Moore. I grew up in a small town but I have always had a passion for community, and providing accessibility to people from all walks of life. Four years of studying at UCLA have taught me how to solve unique nuanced projects to connect with people from all over the world. Now I use these experiences and passions to bring community to the tech world and by coding beautiful, innovative, and accessible websites. I look forward to what we can build together.

About Me

I am UCLA alum who decided to ditch her old career path and dive into tech. I had worked in the Veterinary field for 5 years and even was on track to attending UC Davis School of Veterinary Medicine when I took a hard look at what my future would hold as a vet and decided to make a change. Though I've always loved animals, I knew that path wasn't for me. I've always enjoyed solving puzzles and building different objects growing up and since I had a little knowledge of coding I decided to commit to changing careers and to teach myself how to become a software engineer.

Now I've never been happier with the switch! I love building unique applications that meet the different needs and resources I am presented with. Solving nuanced or novel problems truly is exciting for me and reminds me of all the good things I enjoyed about veterinary medicine.
